# Business Insights & Findings

---

## Revenue Trend Analysis

Revenue peaked during April before gradually declining.

### Insight

Sales momentum weakened after April, suggesting potential seasonality or demand shifts.

---

## Category Analysis

The Set and Kurta categories generate the majority of revenue.

### Insight

These categories are strategic revenue drivers and should receive priority in inventory planning and marketing campaigns.

---

## Fulfillment Analysis

More than 85% of orders were successfully shipped.

### Insight

Operational efficiency is generally strong.

---

## Cancellation Analysis

14% of all orders were cancelled.

### Insight

Further investigation into stockouts, delivery delays, or customer behavior may help reduce cancellations.

---

## Geographic Analysis

A small number of states contribute disproportionately to revenue.

### Insight

Future growth opportunities may exist in underperforming regions.

---

## Customer Segmentation

Retail customers generate nearly all revenue.

### Insight

There is potential opportunity to expand B2B sales channels.
